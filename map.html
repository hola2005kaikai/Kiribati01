Welcome to Tarawa

Tarawa is the capital city of Kiribati, located in the central Pacific Ocean. It serves as the political and administrative heart of the country. The city is known for its vibrant culture, historical significance, and stunning natural beauty. The capital is situated on a small atoll, which plays an essential role in the nation's governance and economy.

A Strong and United Country

Despite being spread across 33 islands scattered over 3.5 million square kilometers of ocean, Kiribati stands as a united nation. The people of Kiribati, from the bustling streets of Tarawa to the remote atolls, share a common bond of heritage, culture, and pride in their country. This unity is reflected in their resilience, as they work together to preserve their way of life, combat climate change, and build a brighter future for their generations to come.
